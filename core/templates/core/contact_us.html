Frequently Asked Questions

Our fair distribution system holds tickets for 10 minutes based on your login time. This ensures first-come-first-served access and prevents unfair advantage from faster internet connections.

Refund policies depend on the event organizer. Check the event details for specific refund terms. Generally, events canceled by organizers result in full refunds.

QR codes are generated automatically after successful booking. If you don't see it in your dashboard, try refreshing the page or contact our support team for assistance.

Use the "Check In" button on your dashboard or present your QR code at the event entrance. Once checked in, your ticket status will be updated and you'll have access to the event.
